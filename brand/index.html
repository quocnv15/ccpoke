Brand Identity

Guidelines for using the ccpoke brand consistently and professionally.

01

Logo

ccpoke uses a mascot bot with cat ears and code bracket eyes. The logo has 4 variants for different contexts.

Filled + Rocket

Hero

Primary variant with rocket flames. Used for favicon, avatar, hero, splash screen.

Filled

Primary

Clean filled variant without flames. Used for small sizes, inline icons, badges.

Outline

Mono

Monochrome variant, used for watermark, loading state, UI icons.

Outline + Rocket

Extended

Outline with rocket flames, used for marketing, splash screen.

Clear Space

Always ensure minimum clear space around the logo equal to the mascot ear height. Do not place text or other elements in this zone.

x

Logo Usage

✓ Do
  • Use the logo on white or dark solid backgrounds
  • Maintain proportions when scaling
  • Use outline variant on dark backgrounds
  • Ensure minimum clear space
✗ Don't
  • Change logo colors
  • Rotate or distort the logo
  • Place logo on complex multicolor backgrounds
  • Add shadow, outline, or effects

04

Mascot Anatomy

Every line on the ccpoke mascot tells a story — a blend of pet-like charm, developer spirit, and the explosive energy of a task well done.

Bear Ears Cute, rounded ears — expressing curiosity and friendliness of the AI agent.
Cat Eyes <> Code bracket symbols — the dev's signature, code-reading eyes.
Bread-shaped Body Rounded, warm shape like bánh mì — fun, approachable, full of energy.
Rocket Flames Speed, energy — the "poke" moment when task is done.
